    Report System

    All reports will be evaluted by rational people, to determine their gravity.

    This can result in 1 of 3 scenarios:

    1 - (The most common scenario) It was a false report and no one will be affected. Unless the false reporter is a serial false reporter, wherein the serial false reporter will be punished.

    2 - The report was justified and the reported account will be punished temporarily (See Below).

    3 - If the report is sufficiently serious, the reported account could be locked out. Leaving their profile desolate, and their e-mail address unusable. This is a rare and unfortunate scenario.

    Some possible punishments

    • Imposing a character limit
    • Renaming your account
    • Locking your customisation to a random color
    • Distorting your UI
